Frequently asked questions

Is RizzIQ free?

Yes, the core app is free to download and use. Optional premium subscriptions unlock additional characters and features.

Who is RizzIQ for?

Guys who want to get better at conversations, texting, and flirting. It's a practice tool — not a dating app and not a replacement for real connections.

Are the AI characters real people?

No. All AI characters in RizzIQ are entirely fictional. They are designed for entertainment and practice only.

What's the age requirement?

RizzIQ is rated 17+ on the App Store. You must be at least 18 years old to create an account and use the app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your Apple ID account settings (Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions). Cancellation takes effect at the end of your billing period.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your conversation data is stored locally on your device. We do not sell personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
